💗 Three Wishes

A Tiny Bouquet
of Wishes

Pick each card ❤️ There is a surprise waiting inside.

🌸 THE WAY YOU LAUGH 🌸

your laugh is one of those sounds that just makes a room feel warmer. wishing you a year full of the kind of moments that bring it out again and again.

🌸 THE WAY YOU SHOW UP🌸

you remember the small things the worry from a Tuesday, the random text on a hard day. it always feels like being seen, not just heard. wishing you the same softness back this year.

🌸 THE YEAR AHEAD 🌸

may this year be kinder than the last. may every new chapter bring you good food, better naps, and people who love you the way you deserve.
